About Go Bus

Go Bus is an Egyptian shareholding company working in the field of public transport under the supervision of the Ministry of Transport since 1998. It is the first private sector company operating in the area of public transportation. Go Bus provides technical solutions by using information and communication technology to improve passenger transportation services and works on developing its benefits for the public to simplify their lives.

Our Vision

Our vision is to provide an outstanding service that exceeds the needs and expectations of our customers to become the easiest and safest solution. We always strive to become a leading company in the field of private transport services by providing comfort and luxury to all users of Go Bus services.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide convenient and safe transportation services that our customers can rely on as a suitable alternative to vehicles. Therefore, we follow our passengers’ needs to enhance our services. Our principle in Go Bus is the comfort and luxury of travelers.

Booking Methods

Luxury & Safety

We care about safety and luxury along with unique service. We equipped our buses with Wi-Fi throughout the road. All Go Bus buses are air-conditioned and equipped with comfortable seats.
Our Aero Bus class launched under the slogan “The luxury of airplane on the ground” has comfortable and spacious seats that move freely with a blanket and pillow to ensure relaxation and restful sleep.
Our Business class has face-to-face seats to provide a great conversation atmosphere for our customers. We offer hot drinks and a fresh meal during the trip so that our customer gets the best luxury services.
Our Elite Plus and Elite Plus Plus classes have a touch screen for each seat with tons of movies, games, and music. You can also charge your mobile phone in your seat.

Terms & Conditions

Online payment

Booking Conditions
  • No more than eight seats can be booked per trip, making a total of 16 seats for the round trip. If more seats are needed, please pay for the 16 tickets, and then make another booking.
  • Payment has to be done before trip time by 4 hours.
  • The booked tickets paid by credit card can be canceled through the website before trip time by 4 hours, for a fee of 15 EGP per ticket.
E-ticket Printing:
  1. After tickets are reserved, the user can print the ticket through the Go Bus website, on regular paper, and this will be the ticket used to ride the bus.
  2. If the user is unable to print the ticket; the user can head to one of the Go Bus offices at the time of the trip and print the ticket using their reference number provided in the confirmation email of the reservation.
  3. If the ticket is available on the phone, it can be shown to the supervisor to board the bus regularly.

Fawry

  • Fawry is a payment service available through banks and a nationwide network of retailers and also Egypt Post offices that enables you to pay for your ticket.
  • First call the hotline 19567, after tickets are reserved. Customer is given a transaction ID, and with this ID you can pay in any stores holding the Fawry machine.
Booking Conditions
  • Customers who book through Fawry have 3 hours to pay their tickets; otherwise, the reservation will be canceled.
  • No more than eight seats can be booked per trip, making a total of 16 seats for the round trip. If more seats are needed, please pay for the 16 tickets, and then make another booking.
  • Payment has to be done before trip time by 2 hours.
  • The original receipt has to be presented to the agent in any of our stations to receive the booked tickets.
  • If the original receipt is lost, a copy of the national ID and order number has to be presented to receive tickets.
